About Us

Catmamma Rescue is organized exclusively for charitable and educational purposes under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. The organization’s mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned, neglected, homeless, and at-risk cats. The organization provides veterinary care, foster placement, nourishment, and socialization to rescued animals until permanent and responsible homes can be secured. Catmamma Rescue also works to reduce feline overpopulation through spay and neuter advocacy, community outreach, and public education promoting humane treatment of animals and responsible pet ownership. In furtherance of its charitable purpose, the organization may collaborate with veterinarians, shelters, volunteers, and community partners to prevent animal suffering and improve animal welfare within the community.

We work with local shelters and good samaritans to give every cat a second chance. All of our cats are spayed/neutered and vaccinated before adoption.

We are 100% donation-funded. Every dollar goes directly to the cats in our care. Thank you for your support! 🧡
